About Me

From the logo on top of my page, you've already gathered that I consider myself to be an editor, writer and communicator, yet those three descriptions merely scratch the surface of who I am.

I was born and raised in St. Augustine, Fla., with two wonderful parents Dinah and Alfi. I have two older brothers who loved to tease and torment me when we were kids. Today, we're extremely close and I couldn't imagine life without them.

My mother is from Ireland and has eight brothers and sisters. As a result, I have a very large family all over the world, from here in the United States to Ireland and all the way to Germany.

The next family function I'll be attending will be my cousin Stacey's wedding this summer in Ireland. As a graduation present to myself, my boyfriend Matt and I will take an extra week off work and travel to France before the wedding.

Yes that's right, I finally graduate in May. I'm proud to say that not only will I be graduating with honors from UF, a top ten journalism school, but I'll also be debt free.

It may have taken me longer than most to earn my degree, but I was determined to graduate on my own terms. I didn't want to bury myself in debt just to get an education. I've worked my way through school and paid for every penny by myself.

I'm fortunate enough to have a job that pays well and is so fun it doesn't feel like work. As a bartender I've had the opportunities to meet interesting people and learn valuable life skills.

Plus, while I was a bartender for TGI Friday's I got to compete in various bartender competitions. How great is it to have a job that encourages you to flip bottles and have fun?

The past two years have taught me that I'm capable of achieving any goal I set my mind to. Working full time while being a full-time student isn't an easy task, but it was definitely worthwhile.

My educational experience has always reminded me of the Robert Frost poem "The Road Not Taken." I chose to take the path less traveled, and that has made all the difference because it has made me who I am.

When I'm not flipping bottles or finishing schoolwork, I'm usually content relaxing at home with my boyfriend Matt and dog Olive. Relaxing generally entails cooking a healthy meal, cuddling up and watching Netflix or playing board games with friends.
