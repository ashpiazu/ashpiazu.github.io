The 5 Ws and H

The key fundamental to telling any story is to answer the five Ws and H: who, what, when, where, why and how. The same principle can be applied when getting to know someone.

As a trained journalist, I couldn't help but to approach my website the same way I would a story. To help you better learn about me, I've broken my website into six sections to answer those questions.

With each click you'll learn something new about me, from my personal history to my professional clips.

As you navigate through the website, I hope all your questions about who I am and what I'm capable of are answered. Enjoy!
