My Plans for the Future

With graduation around the corner, I constantly find myself asking where I'm going to be five years from now.

Although I'm graduating with honors from a top ten journalism school, I don't see myself working as a traditional journalist. I've loved learning the foundation of journalism: asking the right questions, verifying the facts and telling better stories. But at the end of the day, it's not my passion and I want to find work I'm passionate about.

I'm currently seeking an internship or job that will allow me to apply my rigorous journalism training and dazzling interpersonal skills. I'm highly interested in marketing, public relations or corporate communications. Ideally I'd like to work with non-profit organizations and help raise awareness for important social problems.

With my many years of experience in the hospitality industry, I'm also interested in working in event planning, wine and liquor sales, or hotel management. These industries would allow me to continue to meet a wide variety of people and provide excellent customer service.

Essentially I'm open to any job opportunity that requires excellent communication and social skills. I love to challenge myself and learn new things every day.
